Shabb@con is back again with minyanim, meals, and talks during Defcon 32

We are a group of religious Jews who get together at Defcon each year. Shabb@Con's goal is to provide the infrastructure for religious Jews to attend the con by organizing minyanim, seudot, a track of talks on Shabbat afternoon, and more. Itâ€™s a great gathering that can be a welcome respite from the crazy crowds at Defcon and an opportunity to discuss some very unique perspectives on hacking.

This year, due to the ongoing pandemic, things will be more scaled back. Masks will be required during minyanim, but there will be meals this year.

Shabb@Con will be at The Tuscany (BSides LV site). If you take advantage of the BSides LV rate rooms are just $89 during the week and $189 sat/sun with the resort fees waived.

No-Fire Talks

Our famous No-Fire Talks are back again this year! Shabb@con features intimate, off the record talks and discussions about hacking, Judaism, and the unique intersection between the two. Join us in the Shabb@Con suite on Saturday from 14:00 - 1900 to hear from our world renowned speakers.
